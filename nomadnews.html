Introducing NOMAD

an album of exploration and freedom

PreviousNextClose

The exact day I finished Beyond Earth in December of 2018 was the day I started Nomad. Beyond Earth was about creating this beautiful sound that you could easily get lost in. It took you to another place. I wanted to keep that same sound, but apply it in another way. 

While my goal with Beyond Earth was to create a light, magical, and capturing world that melted stress, Nomad is more geared towards a sound of exploration and freedom. Dreamy sounds melt into an urge to do something new and different. I wanted it to be more inspiring and

uplifting than dreamy and otherworldy. I am almost finished with this album. Nomad will be streamable, wearable, and physical sometime this fall, and I can't wait to hear what you all think about this new project of mine.
