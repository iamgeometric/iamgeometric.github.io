ILLUSION (STORY)

    I've always wanted to stand out from the crowd, but I never knew how to do it. I have tried many things that would make me special or allowed me to do something "cool." In a way, they all came around to help me in progressing my music. I have been writing and producing music for over five years now. I always found it to be a way to be emotional without anyone actually seeing me be emotional. I never considered music to be my opportunity to truly stand out until recently, when I started publishing my pieces.

    Even though I had the dream to be something bigger than me, I never attempted it. I stayed in the background. I never found the courage to start the conversation or even join it. Simply through my own lack of courage, I started to feel invisible. It was like I wasn't there. Nobody knew me. People still don't know me. I hide behind a fake mask and a fake personality.  Through trial and error, people acknowledge me now, but they don't know the real me. They know an illusion that I actually laugh. They know an illusion that love's being social.


    I'm still trying to dig myself out of the hole that I dug myself and my music is what is motivating me to do that.
