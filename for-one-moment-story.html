FOR ONE MOMENT (STORY)

A few years back, I lost my grandmother (who I called Granny). It was a really bad moment. We moved into her house to only discover she had cancer months later. It was a long battle, and she didn't win. It really hit me. To this day, I still wish for just one more moment with her. I wrote For One Moment as a way of capturing my feelings about loosing her and what it meant to me.
