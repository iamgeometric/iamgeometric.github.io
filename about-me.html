GEOMETRIC

Six years ago. I opened up a laptop to find an app preinstalled on the awful UI of Windows 8. It was called Music Maker. It gave me loops of basslines, melodies, drums and more to put together and create a song. Music Maker is how I fell in love with creating music. I've since moved onto the FL Studio DAW and a desktop computer. I spend almost all of my free time creating music with a burning passion to be a part of something bigger.
I have just recently started my adventure as a musician. I posted my first single called "For One Moment" in February, but started producing for release in August of 2017, only to later scrap my first album in January of 2018. I have since begun working on a planned series of releases. A four song EP and a sixteen song album that tell my story through emotions provoked by notes instruments, rather than words.
